✦ YBS Manifesto

Our promise to
every Kannadiga.

These are not slogans. These are specific, measurable commitments that Yuva Bharata Sena will fight for — in every ward, every taluk, every district, and every legislature.

Our Five Pillars

The foundations of
Navakarnataka.

01

Zero Corruption

End bribery at every level of government service and public office.

02

Farmers First

Guaranteed minimum price, crop insurance, and farm-to-market infrastructure.

03

Youth Opportunity

Quality education and employment pathways that keep youth in Karnataka.

04

Healthcare for All

Functional government hospitals with medicine, staff, and dignity.

05

Infrastructure Now

Roads, water, power, and sanitation that actually work — across every taluk.

Detailed Commitments

What we will actually do.

Broad promises mean nothing without specifics. Here is what YBS commits to pursuing in government — verifiable, time-bound, and people-driven.

⚖️ Anti-Corruption & Governance

  • Mandatory digital queue and receipt system for all government services
  • Anonymous grievance redressal with 30-day resolution mandate
  • Public disclosure of MLA & official assets every 6 months
  • Independent anti-corruption cell with civilian oversight board
  • Blacklisting of contractors found guilty of bribery
  • RTI response time reduced to 15 working days

🌾 Farmers & Agriculture

  • Guaranteed Minimum Support Price (MSP) for all major crops
  • Crop loss insurance with payout within 45 days of damage
  • Cold-storage infrastructure at taluk level to reduce wastage
  • Zero-interest loans up to ₹2 lakh for small & marginal farmers
  • Farmer producer groups (FPOs) in every taluk within 2 years
  • Solar-powered irrigation pump subsidy for dry-land farmers

🎓 Youth, Education & Employment

  • Every government school to have functioning science lab and library
  • Tablet/device scheme for class 8–12 government school students
  • Skill development centres in every taluk for job-market skills
  • Government job vacancy announcements within 90 days of opening
  • Internship programme linking youth with local government offices
  • Sports infrastructure at panchayat level for every gram panchayat

🏥 Healthcare & Public Health

  • Taluk-level hospitals fully staffed with specialist doctors
  • Essential medicines available free at all government dispensaries
  • Ambulance response time under 20 minutes in urban, 45 in rural areas
  • Annual health check-up camp for every gram panchayat
  • Mental health counsellors at every government college
  • Clean water and sanitation audit for all public health facilities

🛣️ Roads & Infrastructure

  • All village roads (last-mile connectivity) paved within 3 years
  • Pothole complaint resolution under 7 days with photo proof
  • Street lighting in every ward — LED-based, solar where feasible
  • Stormwater drainage audit and upgrade before every monsoon season
  • Public toilet at every bus stop and market cluster
  • Infrastructure spending audits published publicly every quarter

💧 Water, Power & Environment

  • Piped drinking water to 100% of villages within 5 years
  • 24-hour reliable power supply to all households by 2028
  • Reduce urban power cuts to zero during working hours
  • Rainwater harvesting mandatory for all new public buildings
  • Lake and water body restoration in every district
  • Plantation drives — 10 crore trees in 5 years across Karnataka

👩 Women & Social Justice

  • 50% reservation for women in party committees at all levels
  • Fast-track courts for crimes against women in every district
  • Self-help group (SHG) loans at 4% interest through government banks
  • Safe public transport policy — CCTV on all state buses
  • Skill training and livelihood centres for women in all taluks
  • Domestic violence helpline — guaranteed response within 2 hours

🏙️ Urban Development & Housing

  • Affordable housing scheme for low-income urban families
  • Bengaluru Metro Phase extension to all outer ring road nodes
  • Tier-2 city development fund (Mysuru, Hubballi, Mangaluru, Kalaburagi)
  • Smart city services — online property tax, grievance, and permits
  • Slum upgradation with tenure security and basic services
  • Green cover policy — 20% parks and open space in new layouts
