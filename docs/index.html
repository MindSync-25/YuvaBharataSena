Core Issues Pillars
Our Fight

The issues we stand up for, every single day.

Karnataka deserves better. We fight for systemic change on the ground issues that affect real families across every taluk and ward.

✊

Anti-Corruption

Zero tolerance for bribery. We document, expose, and pursue every instance of corruption from ward level to the highest offices.

🛣️

Roads & Infrastructure

Pothole-free roads, drainage systems that work, and infrastructure built for the next generation — not patched up every monsoon.

💧

Water & Sanitation

Clean drinking water is not a privilege. Every Kannadiga deserves reliable water supply and proper sanitation facilities.

🏥

Healthcare Access

Government hospitals that actually function — with medicine, doctors, and dignity for patients and their families.

🎓

Education & Youth

Quality schools, equipped classrooms, and opportunities for Karnataka's youth to build careers without leaving the state.

💡

Electricity & Power

Reliable power for homes, farms, and small businesses — ending the cycle of power cuts that stifle livelihoods.

🌾

Farmers' Welfare

Fair prices for produce, crop insurance that pays out, and a dignified life for the farmers who feed Karnataka.

🏙️

Urban Development

Planned growth for cities, affordable housing, and public transport that makes Bengaluru and tier-2 cities livable.
