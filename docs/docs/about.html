✦ Our Story

Born from the streets
of Karnataka.

Yuva Bharata Sena was founded by people tired of watching corruption go unchecked, infrastructure crumble, and youth leave in search of what their own state could not provide. We chose to stay — and fight.

Who We Are

A movement built on
one principle: accountability.

Yuva Bharata Sena (YBS) is a people-first political movement rooted in the belief that every Kannadiga deserves transparent governance, quality public services, and a dignified life.

We are not career politicians. We are teachers, farmers, engineers, students, shopkeepers, and parents who refused to accept that things cannot change. We built YBS to be the party that answers to its people — every day, not just on election day.

Our work starts at the ward level. Through our app and ground network, members report problems, hold leaders accountable, and build the kind of civic muscle Karnataka needs to grow.

What We Stand For

Six values that guide everything we do.

⚖️

Accountability

Every elected and appointed official must answer to the people. We create systems and processes that make accountability unavoidable — not optional.

🌾

Roots in Rural Karnataka

Karnataka is not just Bengaluru. We invest equally in the farming districts, hill regions, and coastal communities that make our state whole.

🎓

Youth at the Centre

The future belongs to youth. We prioritise education, employment, and pathways to public service that keep Karnataka's talent in Karnataka.

🌐

Transparent Governance

Every rupee of public money must be tracked and reported. We believe in RTI, open data, and civic tech as tools of democracy.

🤝

Inclusive Movement

YBS welcomes every Kannadiga — regardless of caste, religion, gender, or geography. Our diversity is our greatest strength.

🏴

Kannada Pride

We celebrate Kannada language, culture, and heritage. A united Karnataka identity is the foundation on which development must be built.

Mission & Vision

Where we are going —
and how we will get there.

Our Mission

"To hold power accountable at every level of governance in Karnataka."

We achieve this through grassroots organizing, technology-powered civic action, and by fielding honest candidates who represent the real interests of Kannadigas.

Our Vision

"A Karnataka where no citizen fears to report corruption or demand their rights."

A state where quality healthcare, education, and infrastructure are guaranteed — and where every vote truly matters.
