What joining YBS means for you.

📱
Access to the YBS App

Report issues in your area, track resolution status, and communicate directly with your ward and district leaders.

🏆
Rank & Recognition

Earn ranks from Volunteer to State Leader based on your activity, issue resolutions, and referrals. Your work is seen.

🤝
Community & Network

Connect with 50,000+ like-minded Indians who believe in accountability and want to build a better India.

🎓
Training & Skill Building

Access workshops on RTI filing, civic rights, public speaking, and community organizing.

📢
Your Voice Amplified

Issues you report and campaigns you support are escalated through our leaders to the appropriate government authorities.

Ways to Get Involved

Choose your role in the movement.

📋

Booth Committee Member

Lead at the most local level — one booth, one ward. Booth committee members are the backbone of ground-level organizing and voter outreach.

🔥

Youth Wing (Yuva Sena)

Ages 18–35. Our youth wing drives campus outreach, digital campaigns, and innovation within the movement. Be the energy of Nava Bharat.

👩

Women's Wing (Mahila Sena)

Dedicated to women's safety, SHG empowerment, and female leadership. Mahila Sena runs its own programmes and field campaigns.

🏴

State / National Leadership

Take responsibility for organizing an entire state or region. Leadership roles are merit-based and reviewed annually by the national committee.

💻

Digital Volunteer

Social media, graphic design, content creation, data entry — help us spread the message of YBS across India's digital communities.

🌾

Farmer's Cell

Work directly with farming communities — explaining rights, filing complaints, and organizing MSP and crop insurance awareness camps.
